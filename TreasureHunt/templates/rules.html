RULES

  • Each question has a maximum score of 100.
  • Each question has some hints that are going to help you to solve them.
  • Your score is affected by the number of people who have already solved the question
    • If more than 60 people then your score is affected by 8 points.
    • If more than 40 but less than or 60 people then your score is affected by 6 points.
    • If more than 20 people but less than or 40 people then your score is affected by 3 points.
    • If you are among first twenty to solve a question, no deduction.
  • If you attempt more than 30 times in a duration of 2 minutes, you will be timed out.
  • Only one simultaneous session is allowed per user.
  • Ranking is sorted by level, then score and then according to your latest succesful submission time.
  • Some questions may require extra hints, those hints will be posted on SMASH AND LEAP, VERSION 2018, NIT TRICHY Facebook Page.
